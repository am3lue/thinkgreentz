Our Programs

We have a range of programs designed to make a positive impact on the environment and in our communities.

Green Saturdays

A weekly tree-planting initiative that brings together communities to restore local ecosystems.

Think Green Labs

An innovation hub where we develop and test new solutions for environmental challenges.

Agroforestry Partners

A program that supports farmers in adopting sustainable agroforestry practices.

Water & Soil Care

Initiatives focused on conserving water resources and improving soil health.

Clean Energy Literacy

Educational campaigns to promote the use of clean and renewable energy sources.

Circular Clinics

Workshops and events that promote the principles of a circular economy.
